Why Hera Exists

Because women deserve better than generic.

Hera was created out of frustration — with a healthcare system that has spent decades studying male bodies, and a wellness industry that offers one-size-fits-all advice that simply doesn't work for women.

Women's bodies are cyclical. Hormones shift daily. Energy, mood, metabolism, and sleep are all connected — and they change depending on where you are in your cycle, your life stage, and what's happening in your body right now.

Hera was built to change that. To give every woman personalised, evidence-based guidance that meets her exactly where she is — and tells her what to actually do.

Root cause, not symptom management

Rather than masking how you feel, Hera goes deeper — addressing hormones, blood sugar, cortisol, sleep, and nutrition as one connected system.

Built around your cycle, not against it

Your needs change every day of your cycle. Hera's guidance adapts in real time — so what you eat, how you train, and how you rest works with your body, not against it.

With you through every phase of life

From menstruation to fertility, postpartum to perimenopause — Hera grows with you. Because hormonal health doesn't stop, and neither should your support.
